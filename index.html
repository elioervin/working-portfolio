About me

After CoVID 19 shelter-in-place orders swept the country and San Francisco I lost employment. I decided to make a career change, and enrolled in Thinkful’s Engineering Immersion course with an expected Graduation date of April 2021. I’m looking forward to using my skills to promote positive changes within my own community and bringing technology to Organizations that might not have had access otherwise.

I enjoy working in teams and leasing groups and have done so most of my life. I started playing in an orchestra around 9 years old and played sports. Contributing to a group effort whether as a part of the team or a leader of the team is ingrained in my core ideals and concepts of life. I’m super excited for the challenges ahead and can’t wait to get start with a new organization.
